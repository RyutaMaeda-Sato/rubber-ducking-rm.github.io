<h3>プレハブの一部だけが配置される</h3>
<p><strong>問題</strong>: 一部のプレハブが配置されない、または偏りがある。</p>
<p><strong>解決策</strong>: 確率の正規化を確認し、データ検証を追加します。</p>

</span><span class="code-line">    <span class="token keyword">if</span> <span class="token punctuation">(</span>Mathf<span class="token punctuation">.</span><span class="token function">Abs</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span>total <span class="token operator">-</span> <span class="token number">1.0f</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span> <span class="token operator">></span> <span class="token number">0.01f</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span>

</span><span class="code-line">        <span class="token keyword">for</span> <span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token class-name"><span class="token keyword">int</span></span> i <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token number">0</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span> i <span class="token operator">&#x3C;</span> spawnProbabilities<span class="token punctuation">.</span>Length<span class="token punctuation">;</span> i<span class="token operator">++</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span>

</span><span class="code-line">        <span class="token punctuation">}</span>
